My Therapeutic Approach

I believe in a collaborative, culturally-sensitive approach that honors your unique experiences while utilizing evidence-based practices for optimal outcomes.

1

Evidence-Based Practice

Utilizing scientifically-supported therapeutic methods including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and trauma-informed approaches.

2

Cultural Sensitivity

Understanding and respecting the role of culture, identity, and background in shaping your experiences and incorporating this into treatment planning.

3

Collaborative Partnership

Working together as partners in your healing journey, with you as the expert on your own experience and me providing professional guidance and support.
